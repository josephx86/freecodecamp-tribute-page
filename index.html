Diana

- Scorn of the Moon

Diana is a Lunari warrior who is the living embodiment of the silver moon's power. She fights clad in shimmering armor the color of winter snow at night. Here are a few interesting facts about her:

  • She was born during a storm on Mount Targon.
  • Her mother passed away soon after Diana was born.
  • The name 'Diana' was given by hunters from a Solari Temple who found her the day after she was born.
  • As one of the Solari, Diana was raised to venerate the sun in all its aspects.
  • She learned the legends of the sun and was trained by the Ra-Horak, the warrior templars of the Solari.
  • Contrary to what the Solari elders taught, Diana grew interested in moonlight.
  • Diana found moonlight entrancing and beautiful in a way the harsh sun glaring down the mountain could never match.
  • As the years went by, Diana found hereself ever at odds with the Solari elders and their teachings.
  • She couldn't help but question al she was told, always suspecting there was more that went unsaid in every teaching, as though what she was being taught was willfully incomplete.
  • As she grew, Diana's sense of isolation only became stronger as childhood friends distanced themselves from here.
  • With every passing day, Diana felt more alone and more certain that some vital aspect of her life remained unfulfilled.
  • While sweeping the temple library as punishment for arguing with one of her elders, Diana discovered the partially burned pages of an ancient manuscript.
  • From the fragmentary text, Diana learned of an all-but-extinct group known as the Lunari, wose faith saw the moon as a source of life and balance.
